The problem right now

Almost nobody's disclosures actually hold up.

01

The bar moved

Platform "Paid Partnership" tags alone no longer satisfy the FTC — creators need the tag plus clear in-caption language, and enforcement now pulls your briefs and contracts as evidence.

02

Reposts break compliance

A compliant Instagram post can lose its disclosure entirely the moment it's re-uploaded to TikTok or Reels — and that creates a brand-new violation nobody's watching for.

03

No documentation, no defense

Spot-checks in a Slack channel don't hold up in an investigation. The FTC wants dated, structured proof that you monitored — not that you meant to.
